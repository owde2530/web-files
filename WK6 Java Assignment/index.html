Supermega: An Introduction

Supermega is a YouTube channel featuring Matt Watson and Ryan Magee. The channel primarily features let's plays, a podcast, as well as live action skits and vlogs. Their channel features a dry and ironic sense of humor, perpetually blurring the line between being serious and not. The channel currently has about 940K subscribers. Its podcast series and let's plays number in the thousands.

Notable Quotes:

Watch Supermega if you have an extremely ironic sense of humor - trust me, they really do skate the line often, and it's simulatenously funny, confusing, and delightfully annoying.
