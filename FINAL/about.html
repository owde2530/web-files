Owen deGroot: An Introduction

Owen deGroot is a senior year university student at CU Boulder. He is also a screenwriter, animator, filmmaker, photographer, and writer. Screenwriting is deGroot's main focus: Owen has written dozens of short film screenplays, as well as several feature length and TV pieces as well. Owen has also shot and produced several short films in and outside of school. These works include live action and animated pieces. deGroot also enjoys taking photos of a variety of subjects, both on digital and analog formats.


Some fun facts:

  • Ate shark once in 2011.
  • Been to 5.3 foreign countries!
  • Can hold breath for 37 seconds!

Currently, Owen is working on many projects. He was lucky enough to have served as a 2D animator for the upcoming feature film, The Boardinghouse Reach (dir. Geoff Marslett). Exact release date for the film is unknown, but it is set for a 2022 release. Additionally, deGroot is also currently working on a new animation for his CINE 3400 class (working title: Don't Get Your Head in The Clouds!) It should be coming out in January 2021!
