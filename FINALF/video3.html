Cereal Killers (actor, 2021)

(full video link - videos above are previews only)

At the beginning of my 3400 film production class, students were assigned to practice film set roles on a small group project. So, while most people decided to take on technical roles like DP, Sound, I took on the role of an actor this project. And this is where I fit in in the general scheme of Cereal Killers. In particular, I play the role of Lester, the guy in the white collared shirt. In this short film, a group of gangsters hotly discuss their favorite cereals, while failing to notice or "take care of" their rival gang target. I'm not usually an actor, but I had a fun time acting in this role, and I'm glad to include it here in this collection (for the record, my favorite cereal IS in fact Cinnamon Toast Crunch.)
