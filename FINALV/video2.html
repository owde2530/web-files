Carlos (writer/director, 2021)

(full video link - videos above are previews only)

For my current CINE 3400 film production class, we were given an interesting assignment: listen in on a real-life conversation, and, from this snippet, make a short narrative film. When writing, I came across something intriguing as inspiration. Carlos is inspired by this one time, while walking about on the Hill, I heard two different pairs of girls talking about the same boy. I assumed it was just a coincidence (he had a common name, too, like Jack or the like.) But, I thought it'd be funny if this mysterious suitor was an internet catfish, scamming these poor, innocent girls. So, thus, the plot of Carlos was born. Carlos follows a random onlooker getting wrapped into a larger scheme of catfishing, love, betrayal, and intrigue. CW: Not for the faint of heart.
